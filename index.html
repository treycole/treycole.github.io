Biography

I'm a PhD student at Rutgers University specializing in condensed matter theory. My research focuses on quantum materials, particularly those exhibiting non-trivial topological properties. I develop computational tools and perform numerical simulations to uncover additional insights into these materials. Most of my work is open source and available on my GitHub.
