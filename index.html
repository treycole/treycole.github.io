Biography

I'm a PhD student at Rutgers University studying condensed matter theory, focusing on quantum materials, topological materials and electronic structure calculations. Prior to this I completed my M.Sc. at TU Delft with an emphasis in Quantum Devices and Quantum Computing, and a B.S. at West Virginia University with an emphasis in Computational Physics. I enjoy coding tools for scientific investigation. You can find my projects on my GitHub.
